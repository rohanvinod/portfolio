About Me

I bought a mac and downloaded Xcode, which was an application that allowed me to build ios apps and run them on my device. I started with a very simple application, a multplication tester. The goal for this app was to test the simple multiplication tables from 1 to 10, and help elementary school kids learn them as quickly as possible. I had a few beta testers test the app and recieved a ton of feedback. I then published my app on the App Store and decided to create some other apps as well.
