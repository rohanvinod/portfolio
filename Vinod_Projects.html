AppInventor Project

In Computer Science Pricipals(CSP), I modified an app in App Inventor called PaintPot. PaintPot is a drawing app that allows the user to draw something on a screen with the touch of a finger. Before, the app only included 3 colors, Red, Blue, and Green. We wanted to expand the range of colors used so we made 4 more colors, Pink, Orange, Black, and Yellow. Next, we wanted to add more strokes for the lines to provide more variety in the art created by the user. This proved to be a difficult task because it was hard to change the actual look of the line, so we just made a slider to allow the user to choose the thickness of the line that they want to draw with.

Cookies On the Web

This was a research project dedicated to learning all about web cookies. I talked about what cookies were and what their function was. Cookies are tiny packets of data stored on browsers that contain simple infpormation such as logins and passowrds. Why do you need cookies? Well, cookies can make it much easier to browse the web. For instance, say you want to login to a website but forgot your passcode. With cookies, the passsword will automatically fill and you won't need to remember your passsword the next time you login to the same website. Cookeis just make browsing very convineient. However one downide to cookies are that they can be easily intercepted by hackers and the data can become compromised. Cookies are not very secure.
